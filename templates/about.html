About Coimbatore Bazaar

Welcome to Coimbatore Bazaar, your one-stop online shop for the latest and greatest electronic gadgets! We are a passionate team dedicated to bringing you high-quality products at competitive prices, right here from the heart of Coimbatore.

Our mission is to provide a seamless and enjoyable shopping experience, offering everything from smart home devices and audio accessories to wearable tech and entertainment gadgets. We believe in innovation, customer satisfaction, and building a trusted community for tech enthusiasts.

Our Vision

To become the leading e-commerce platform for electronics in Coimbatore and beyond, known for our diverse product range, excellent customer service, and commitment to technological advancement.

This project was developed by an intern as a learning exercise to gain hands-on experience with Flask, MongoDB, and web development principles. It showcases core e-commerce functionalities like product display, categorization, shopping cart management, and basic CRUD operations. Feedback and suggestions are always welcome!
